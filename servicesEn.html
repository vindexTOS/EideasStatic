IoT

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a revolutionary concept that refers to the network of physical objects, devices, vehicles, buildings, and other items embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity, allowing them to collect and exchange data over the internet. At its core, IoT aims to enhance efficiency, convenience, and productivity by enabling seamless communication and collaboration between devices, systems, and users. By integrating the physical and digital worlds, IoT empowers businesses, industries, and individuals with unprecedented insights, control, and automation.

PCB Design
